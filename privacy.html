Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2025  ·  Last updated: 1 January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. Zeitly complies with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (revFADP).

01Who We Are

Zeitly is a time-exchange marketplace operated from St. Gallen, Switzerland. For the purposes of Swiss data protection law, Zeitly is the data controller responsible for your personal data.

Contact: legal@zeitly.app

02Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the Service:

Data Why we collect it
Name & email Account creation and authentication
Profile photo Displayed on your profile and to other users
Location (city level) Showing services near you
Service listings Displayed publicly on the marketplace
Messages Communication between users
Exchange records Tracking credit transfers and history
Reviews Building trust and reputation on the platform
Device & session data App stability, analytics (via Firebase)
Feedback messages Improving the Service

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health information, political opinions, or financial account details.

03Legal Basis for Processing

Under the revFADP, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide the Service you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve the Service, ensure security, and prevent abuse
  • Consent — for optional features such as location access, which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legal obligation — where required by Swiss law

04How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Operate and improve the Zeitly platform
  • Enable exchanges and credit transfers between users
  • Display your profile and services to other users
  • Send transactional communications (exchange updates, account notifications)
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties.

05Third-Party Services

Zeitly uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Storage, Analytics) — infrastructure and data storage. Firebase servers may be located within the EU/EEA. Google LLC is certified under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.
  • Apple Sign In — optional authentication method. Apple processes only the data required for sign-in.

We do not share your data with any other third parties except as required by law.

06Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and public content are removed from view immediately
  • Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Exchange records may be retained for up to 7 years where required by Swiss accounting law
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

07Your Rights

Under the revFADP, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Erasure Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
Objection Object to processing based on legitimate interests
Withdrawal Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@zeitly.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) at edoeb.admin.ch.

08Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (TLS), Firebase security rules that restrict access to your own data, and regular security reviews.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at legal@zeitly.app.

09Children

Zeitly is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The current version is always available at this URL.
